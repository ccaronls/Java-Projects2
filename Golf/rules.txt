The game of Golf

Wiki: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Golf_(card_game)

DESCRIPTION

Golf is a card game that supports between 2 and 8 players.  The deck consists of 2 complete 52 card decks
as well as 8 Jokers.  The object of the game is to get the LEAST amount of points.  It is possible to have negative points.

SETUP

A dealer is chosen and 8 cards are dealt face down to each player starting with player to left of dealer.  All Cards remain face 
down.   Players arrange their cards into 2 rows of 4 cards placed atop each other as shown in diagram 1.

SCORING

Jokers are worth -5.  All Kings are worth 0.  Queens and Jacks are worth 10, all other cards are worth face value.  
Paired cards of the same Rank are worth 0.

Example: The following hand has value 0+0+3+0-5+10+6+5=18.  The twos cancel each other since they are paired, so they are worth 0.
         Kings are always worth 0.  Jokers are always worth -5.
