E-Signing is a valid legal document according to the Federal ESIGN Act of 2000. In short, electronic documentation is as good as old fashioned paper. The system we provide is similar to those you use at the grocery store or when accepting deliveries. Electronic forms have a number of advantages in addition to simplifying processes: - The final document is more secure than paper because it will persist on multiple servers. - Forgeries are easy to detect. - You can alway choose to print out the emailed form for your records at a later time.
